ABOUT ME

Work History

Before being a student at DBC I was a software engineer at Bespoke Innovations in San Francisco (now owned by 3D Systems). I worked on software for 3D patient scanners. After working at Bespoke for two consecutive summers I interned as a Toy Inventor in the IDEO ToyLab. There I did electronics and embedded systems programming for toys.
