Favorite Websites Wireframe

Brennon Williams, Dev Bootcamp Week One

Hacker News

This webpage solves the need for high-quality, curated tech news articles. There are so many new articles published everyday that keeping track of all of them is near impossible. Hacker News lets users decide what is relevant, and it dynamically displays the top links on its page. The biggest visual challenge is getting the user to see the top links as soon as possible. This is achieved through a minimalist, simple list of the top links.

Apple

Apple.come gives customers the latest Apple news, information on their products, and the ability to buy Apple products from the web. The challenge is allowing all three of these different needs to be met on one homepage in an aesthetically appeasing way.

Hulu

Hulu solves the need for free access to TV and movies in a web browser. The major challenge is showing the wide offering of content without cluttering the homepage or overwhelming the user.
