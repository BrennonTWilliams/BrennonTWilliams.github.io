Personal Site plan

For the personal site I want to leverage white space as much as possible. The design should be minimalistic and clean, with an emphasis on typography. I want the colorscheme to be blue/light blue/grey/white because, well, I like blue. I want a centered header with my picture in the top left corner (in the style of today's trendy tech blogs).
